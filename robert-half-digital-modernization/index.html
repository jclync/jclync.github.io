Robert Half

Digital Service Products Modernization

In summer 2022, I worked a Business Transformation Intern at Robert Half, and I took on various initiatives to improve the user experience of the Candidate and Candidates who use Robert Half services.
Besides working on reinventing the existing Client and Candidate digital service products, I also spearheaded a project with two other design interns to brainstorm and plan Robert Half's inaugural Usability Day event.

Timeframe

June 2022 - August 2022

Key Contributions

  • Product Design
  • Client Communication
  • User Research

Client Context

UW CERSE is a team of researchers who focus on conducting research to improve equity and broaden representation in STEM fields in higher education. Their research is currently focusing on PNW LSAMP (the Pacific Northwest Louis Stokes Alliance for Minority Participation), which includes the University of Washington, Washington State University, Oregon State University, Portland State University, and Boise State University. From their research, they've observed that efforts related to improving STEM equity are often siloed, hard to locate, and sometimes provide gaps in the types of support that students need.
