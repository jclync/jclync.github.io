Social Media Graphics

A collection of social media graphics and merchandise apparel that I designed for UW Asian American InverVarsity (UW AAIV) and YiFang Taiwan Fruit Tea.

UW AAIV

UW AAIV is a Christian fellowship at the University of Washington that seeks to empower Asian Americans to further God's kingdom on campus. As a designer, I collaborated closely with other Creative Team members to create graphics promoting upcoming events on the club's social media platforms and for display use during announcements at the weekly large group meetings. Additionally, I designed and assembled photo backdrops for the club's annual Christmas party and created merchandise apparel for club members.

Duration

September 2021 - June 2023

YiFang Taiwan Fruit Tea

YiFang Taiwan Fruit Tea is a boba drink franchise originating from Taiwan, with multiple storefronts across the US. In my role as a social media designer, I collaborated closely with my managers to create engaging graphics for the store's social media platforms. These designs were aimed at promoting events, introducing new menu items, and enhancing in-store displays.
